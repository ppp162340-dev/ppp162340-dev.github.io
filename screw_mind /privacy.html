Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application Screw Mind - Twist Puzzle ("App"). By downloading, installing, or using our App, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

What Information Do We Collect?

We gather only necessary information to enhance your gaming experience and ensure the App’s smooth operation. The types of information we may collect include:

  • Account & Profile Information: When you sign in through Google, Apple ID, or Facebook, we receive basic profile details such as your registered email address, display name, and profile picture. This data is solely used to create your unique account, sync progress across devices, and personalize your in-app experience.

  • Device & Technical Identifiers: We collect your device’s Advertising Identifier (IDFA for iOS, GAID for Android) to prevent fraudulent activities (e.g., fake reward claims) and collaborate with ad partners. We also gather your IP address, device model, operating system version, screen resolution, time zone, default language, and network type (WiFi/cellular) to optimize the App for different devices and resolve compatibility issues.

  • Usage & Interaction Data: We track how you engage with the App, including twist puzzle completion rates, time spent on each level, preferred difficulty settings, use of hint tools, and interactions with in-app ads or reward tasks. This helps us refine game mechanics, balance difficulty, and introduce user-centric features.

  • Error & Performance Reports: If the App crashes, freezes, or encounters technical glitches, we automatically collect crash logs, error codes, and device performance data (e.g., CPU usage, memory status) to diagnose and fix issues promptly.

  • Third-Party Optional Data: Our ad partner (AdMob) may request access to information about other apps installed on your device when you first launch the App. This request is optional—you can allow or deny it without impacting core gameplay. We do not access, store, or process this information; it is solely handled by AdMob per their privacy policy.

How Do We Use Your Information?

The information we collect is used exclusively for legitimate purposes related to the App:

  • To sync your twist puzzle progress, achievements, and account settings across multiple devices.

  • To personalize your gaming experience (e.g., suggesting levels matching your skill level, unlocking themes based on preferences).

  • To display non-intrusive, targeted advertisements that align with your interests (via your Advertising Identifier).

  • To respond to your support inquiries, resolve technical issues, and follow up on feedback.

  • To communicate important updates (e.g., new puzzle packs, feature launches) or changes to this Privacy Policy.

  • To analyze usage patterns, identify popular features, and improve the App’s performance, stability, and gameplay.

How Is Your Information Shared?

We prioritize your privacy and only share information in limited, necessary circumstances:

  • Your Advertising Identifier may be shared with trusted ad partners (e.g., AdMob) to deliver targeted ads and measure ad performance. These partners are contractually obligated to protect your data and may not use it for unrelated purposes.

  • We may share anonymized, aggregated data (e.g., overall puzzle completion rates, device model distribution) with third-party analysts for research and App improvement—this data cannot identify individual users.

  • Service providers assisting with App operations (e.g., cloud storage, crash reporting tools) may access necessary information but are bound by strict confidentiality agreements to protect it.

  • We may disclose information if required by law (e.g., court subpoena, regulatory request) or to protect our legal rights, safety, or the safety of other users.

Data Security

We implement robust security measures to safeguard your information:

  • All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using TLS 1.3 protocol.

  • Personal information stored on our servers is protected with AES-256 encryption.

  • Access to sensitive data is restricted to authorized personnel only, who are required to sign confidentiality agreements.

  • Regular security audits and vulnerability scans are conducted to identify and address potential risks.

While we strive to protect your data, no security system is entirely foolproof. We cannot guarantee absolute security, but we continuously update our measures to mitigate emerging threats.

How Long Do We Keep Your Information?

We retain your information only for as long as necessary:

  • Account and profile information is kept while your account is active; it is deleted immediately upon your account deletion request.

  • Game progress and achievement data are stored to maintain your gameplay experience and are deleted when you request account deletion.

  • Technical data, usage analytics, and error reports are retained for up to 9 months—after which they are anonymized or deleted.

  • Information required for legal compliance (e.g., tax records, fraud prevention) is kept for the duration of the legal obligation.

You can request complete deletion of your account and all associated data at any time through one of the following methods:

  • Via the App: Navigate to "Settings" > "Account & Privacy" and select "Delete My Account"—follow the on-screen prompts to confirm.

  • Via email: Send a request to TylerBanks76447@icloud.com with the subject line "Screw Mind - Twist Puzzle Account Deletion Request." Include your registered email address (linked to your App account) for verification.

We will process your request within 14 business days and send a confirmation email once deletion is complete. Please note that deleted data cannot be recovered.
